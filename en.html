Мы являемся победителями гранта Федерального агентства по делам молодежи на выставку произведений современного искусства.

Это образовательный проект нового поколения архитектурной практики, разрабатываемый с использованием новых инструментов концептуального программирования для написания сценариев и обработки сложных поверхностей и неожиданных конструкций.

Результатом проекта станет экспозиция произведений современного искусства, которая состоится в мае 2019 года, а также подготовка второй части экспозиции, открытие которой запланировано на конец 2019 года.

We are winners of a grant by the Federal Agency for Youth Affairs for the exposition of works of modern art.

This is an educational project of the new generation of architectural practice being developed using new tools of conceptual programming for scripting and processing of complex surfaces and non-conventional structures.

The project will result in an exposition of works of modern art, which will be held in May 2019, as well as in preparation of the second exposition, planned for opening in the end of 2019.

The opening of the Architectural Collaborations Modern Art Exhibition — 4 works of modern art — will be held at the Gallery of the Union of Russian Artists in Chelyabinsk on April 29, 2019.

Open Competition with iGeo

How to Take Part in the Competition with iGeo?

To participate in the Architectural Collaborations competition, you need to think up a geometrical surface for one of the 10 educational tasks of iGeo Tutorials. Currently, iGeo can read the majority of the types of geometries in Wavefront OBJ (.obj) and in Rhinoceros 3dm version 4.0 (.3dm).

Once you place the file into the Processing sketch data folder, you can open the file by re-naming the educational file in IG.open() method. Launch the Processing sketch with Ctrl + R, and you will see the process of the algorithm execution. Try changing the numerical variables in the "for"" expression, placed in squiggle brackets { ... } in the Panelization Examples section, or in the lines between "void setup(){" ... "}" and in the line "double dirScale = 0.1; //1.0;" of the dirScale variable in the statement of PanelAgent class in the Agent-Based Panelization Algorithm section. Go and experiment! Find an interesting combination and save in *.3dm by pressing Ctrl + S  during sketching. Present your visualization — the final presentation of the project — a work of modern art in a public space.

Panelization Examples — Example3, Example5
Agent-Based Panelization Algorithm — 1, 2, 3, 4, 5, 9, 10, 13

Install Processing and iGeo. In the Processing, place the data files into the "data" folder, inside the sketch folder. To do that, go to "Sketch > Add File..." and select the input file, then the Processing will automatically create the "data" subfolder and will copy the input file into the "data" folder.

E-mail the *.3dm file and your work in the highest resolution to amirgumarov@hotmail.com. Indicate your name and the name of the project chosen for visualization in your signature. The contest works will be accepted starting now and till 10:59 Moscow time on March 24th.
